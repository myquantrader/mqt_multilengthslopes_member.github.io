MQT_MultiLengthSlopes

Precision Slope Analysis Across Multiple Bar Lengths

MQT_MultiLengthSlopes displays real‑time slope angles (in degrees) for both High and Low prices, with optional multi‑bar slope comparisons, automatic color coding, flat‑slope filtering, and CSV export for advanced analysis.

Key Features

High & Low Slope Degrees

Displays precise slope angles for both High and Low price movement on each bar.

Multi‑Bar Slope Comparison

Calculate slopes between any two historical bars for deeper structural analysis.

Automatic Direction Coloring

Green for positive slope, red for negative slope — instantly readable.

Flat‑Slope Filtering

Automatically hide near‑flat slopes using a configurable degree threshold.

Text & Line Control

Independently toggle degree labels and slope lines for a clean chart.

CSV Export

Export slope data (High, Low, additional slopes) for spreadsheet or research use.

FAQ

Does this repaint?

No. Slopes are calculated using completed bars.

Can I limit slopes to the current bar?

Yes. Enable “Only Current Bar” to show slopes only on the most recent bar.

Can I compare arbitrary bars?

Yes. Additional slopes allow you to specify any two bar offsets.

Where is the CSV saved?

Documents → NinjaTrader 8 → export
